Biography

Kristoffer Carlsson is a Ph.D student at the Material and Computational Mechanics department at Chalmers University of Technology. His research interests include material modeling of crystal plasticity and homogenization of the response to the macro scale.

He also enjoys working on open software projects, in particular, the Julia language project.
